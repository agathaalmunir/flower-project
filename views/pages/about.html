More convenient than a cross-country delivery and more personal than a stock photo


FlowerCode is a free application that allows you to create personalized flower bouquets and send them virtually to loved ones.


Designing a bouquet is fast and easy. Choose from four different categories to build your bouquet and see which style matches your recipient and/or the occasion the most.  Add a note if you'd like, and instantly email a virtual bouquet without leaving the site.


How We Started


The idea for FlowerCode originated during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. With social distancing, lockdowns, and both the direct and indirect effects of the pandemic, we needed a little something to brighten our day and show others we care from a safe distance.


As the pandemic thankfully slows to end, this application still bridges the distance during the times we are apart from our friends and family. It can be a loving surprise to your long-distance partner, child who's studying abroad, loved one in the hospital, or even your best friend who lives just a block away.
